About ResQHer

ResQHer is a dedicated women’s safety platform designed to provide quick, reliable, and accessible support in times of need. With features like one-tap SOS alerts, real-time live location sharing, and verified helpline numbers, our mission is to ensure that help is always just a click away.

We believe safety is not just a privilege—it’s a basic right. ResQHer is more than just an app; it’s a digital companion that empowers women with confidence and peace of mind, whether they’re traveling, working late, or simply stepping out alone.
